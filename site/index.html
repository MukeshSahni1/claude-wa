FAQ

Will fans know it's an AI?

If they ask directly — yes, it's honest ("I'm the AI twin!"). Most fans love that you have one. You can also say it upfront in your bio: "chat with my AI 💜".

What if it says something wrong?

You set the rules — what it can talk about, what it plugs, what it avoids. It refuses weird requests politely, and you can pause it or jump into any chat yourself, anytime.

Do I need a new SIM?

No. We can run your twin on a number we provide — you just pin the link. (You can use your own number later if you want.)

How much does it cost?

Early creators get it free while we polish it. After that, a simple monthly price — less than what one brand post pays you.
